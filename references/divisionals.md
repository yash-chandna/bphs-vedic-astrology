# Divisional Charts — Shodashavarga (BPHS Ch. 6–7)

Each divisional chart is a sub-division of the rasi chart used to assess specific life areas with greater precision. Never interpret a divisional in isolation — always read against the D1 (Rasi) chart first.

## The 16 Shodashavarga Charts

| Chart | Name | Division | Purpose |
|---|---|---|---|
| D1 | Rasi (Natal Chart) | 1 | Overall life, body, personality — primary chart for all readings |
| D2 | Hora | 2 | Wealth and finances; Sun hora = male/gold; Moon hora = female/silver |
| D3 | Drekkana | 3 | Siblings, courage, co-born; also death/afterlife in some chapters |
| D4 | Chaturthamsa | 4 | Immovable property, fortune from land and homes |
| D7 | Saptamsa | 7 | Children and grandchildren — primary chart for progeny |
| D9 | Navamsa | 9 | Spouse, marriage, dharma, soul's nature — most important divisional |
| D10 | Dasamsa | 10 | Career, profession, status, actions in the world (karma) |
| D12 | Dwadashamsa | 12 | Parents — father, mother; ancestry and genetic inheritance |
| D16 | Shodashamsa | 16 | Vehicles, comforts, happiness from conveyances |
| D20 | Vimshamsa | 20 | Spiritual practice, upasana, religious merit |
| D24 | Chaturvimshamsa | 24 | Education, learning, knowledge attainment |
| D27 | Nakshatramsa | 27 | Strength, vitality, physical prowess |
| D30 | Trimshamsa | 30 | Misfortunes, evils, diseases; female horoscopy |
| D40 | Khavedamsa | 40 | Auspicious/inauspicious effects — maternal lineage |
| D45 | Akshavedamsa | 45 | General well-being — paternal lineage |
| D60 | Shashtiamsha | 60 | Past life karma, subtlest level of fate — highest weight in BPHS; requires accurate birth time |

## Key Navamsa (D9) Rules

**Vargottama**: A planet in the same sign in both D1 and D9 is greatly strengthened. Even a debilitated Vargottama planet gains significant power (BPHS Ch. 7).

**D1 strong, D9 weak**: Initial promise that fades or is not fully realized.

**D1 weak, D9 strong**: Better than it appears — results improve over time, especially in the planet's dasha.

**Atmakaraka in Navamsa**: The planet occupying the Navamsa lagna reveals the native's deeper spiritual direction (BPHS).

## Varga Visesha — Special Dignity Levels (BPHS Ch. 7)

When a planet is in its own sign or exaltation across multiple vargas:

| Name | Vargas required | Meaning |
|---|---|---|
| Parijata | 2 | Comfortable life |
| Uttama | 3 | Good standard of living |
| Gopura | 4 | Nobility, leadership |
| Simhasana | 5 | Authority and power |
| Paravata | 6 | Great independence and happiness |
| Devaloka | 7 | Divine pleasures, high spiritual merit |
| Brahmaloka | 8 | Very rare — supreme attainment |
| Srikantha | 9 | Extremely powerful, blessed |
| Mridanga | 10 | Fame lasting beyond one life |
