# Career & Profession (BPHS Ch. 10, 24, Dasamsa)

Career analysis in BPHS integrates the 10th house (karma/action), the Dasamsa (D10), planetary significations, and the current dasha period. All statements must cite BPHS.

## Primary Indicators of Career

### 10th House (Karma Bhava)
The 10th house and its lord are the foundation of career analysis.

- **10th lord's placement**: The house it occupies shows the field of work
- **Planets in the 10th**: Colour the profession with their significations
- **10th lord in kendra/trikona**: Strong, successful career
- **10th lord in dusthana (6,8,12)**: Career obstacles, service sector, or hidden work
- **10th lord exalted/own sign**: Professional excellence and recognition

### Natural Karakas for Career (BPHS Ch. 32)
Sun, Mercury, Jupiter, and Saturn are the four co-significators of the 10th house. Each adds a layer:
- **Sun**: Authority, government, leadership, father's profession influence
- **Mercury**: Trade, communication, accounting, intellect-based work
- **Jupiter**: Teaching, law, finance, advisory roles, dharmic professions
- **Saturn**: Service, labour, discipline-based work, masses, agriculture, iron/metal industries

### Lagna Lord
The lagna lord's involvement with the 10th house or 10th lord strengthens career drive and personal investment in profession.

## Dasamsa (D10) — Career Chart

The D10 is the primary divisional chart for career assessment. Never read D10 in isolation — always cross-reference with D1.

Key rules:
- **D10 lagna and its lord**: The native's overall professional identity and approach
- **D10 10th house**: The peak of professional achievement in this chart
- **Sun in D10**: Government, authority, recognition
- **Strong D10 despite weak D1 10th house**: Career improves with effort over time
- **Weak D10 despite strong D1 10th**: Initial promise that struggles to materialise

## Profession by Planetary Rulership

Use the planet(s) most strongly connected to the 10th house (by occupation, aspect, or lordship) to identify the professional domain:

| Ketu | Research, occult, spirituality, medicine (alternative), technical precision |

## Timing Career Events (Dasha)

Career changes, promotions, and setbacks correlate with dasha periods. Key triggers:

- **10th lord's Mahadasha**: Most important period for career — major professional chapter
- **Dasha of planets in the 10th house**: Activates career significations directly
- **Saturn Mahadasha/Antardasha**: Discipline, delays, or sustained career building (slow but lasting)
- **Sun period**: Government recognition, authority, or role involving leadership
- **Rahu period**: Sudden career changes, foreign opportunities, unconventional rise
- **Ketu period**: Detachment from career, spirituality, research, or technical specialisation

## Career Reading Output Format

Structure career readings as:

**Section 1 — 10th House Analysis**: Sign, planets in 10th, aspects on 10th, 10th lord placement and strength.

**Section 2 — Dasamsa (D10)**: D10 lagna, 10th house in D10, key planets in D10 and how they modify D1 indications.

**Section 3 — Planetary Significations**: Which planets dominate the career and what fields they indicate.

**Section 4 — Dasha Timing**: Current and upcoming dasha periods relevant to career — changes, growth, or consolidation.

**Section 5 — Summary**: Synthesised career direction with BPHS citations for all statements.
