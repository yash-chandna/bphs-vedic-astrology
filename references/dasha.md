## Bhukti (Sub-period) Duration

Bhukti duration = (Mahadasha years × Bhukti planet years) / 120

Example: Venus Mahadasha / Saturn Bhukti = (20 × 19) / 120 = 3 years 2 months

## General Dasha Phala Principles (BPHS Ch. 47–50)

| Condition | Result |
|---|---|
| Dasha lord is benefic in kendra/trikona | Prosperity, health, advancement |
| Dasha lord in dusthana (6,8,12) or malefic owning dusthana | Disease, obstacles, loss, separation |
| Dasha lord debilitated (no Neecha Bhanga) | Difficulty, frustration, failure of significations |
| Dasha lord exalted or in own sign | Excellent results proportional to Shadbala and house position |
| Dasha lord combust | Reduced results, confusion |
| Mahadasha and Antardasha lords are mutual enemies | Conflict, contradiction, mixed results |
| Mahadasha and Antardasha lords are friends | Synergistic, harmonious results |
| Dasha lord is Maraka (lord of 2nd or 7th) in appropriate age window | Serious health threats |

## Dasha Lagna

Each planet's dasha activates the house it occupies as a temporary lagna. Interpret results from both natal lagna AND dasha lagna simultaneously.
