# Yoga Definitions (BPHS Ch. 35–39)

Yogas are special planetary combinations that modify the chart reading. Assess each yoga's actual strength — a yoga with weak or combust planets is theoretical. Always cite the specific BPHS chapter when reporting a yoga.

## Raja Yogas (BPHS Ch. 36) — Power, authority, success

| Yoga | Rule | Strength |
|---|---|---|
| **Raja Yoga (Kendra-Trikona)** | Lords of a kendra (1,4,7,10) and trikona (1,5,9) conjoin, exchange, or aspect each other | Strong |
| **Dharma-Karmadhipati Yoga** | Lords of 9th and 10th conjoin, exchange, or mutually aspect — one of the most powerful Raja Yogas | Strong |
| **Viparita Raja Yoga** | Lords of all three dusthanas (6,8,12) placed in each other's houses or conjoin in a dusthana, without association with lagna/trikona lords | Strong |
| **Neecha Bhanga Raja Yoga** | Debilitation is cancelled by: (1) dispositor in kendra, (2) planet exalted in that sign in kendra, (3) lord of debilitation sign aspects debilitated planet, (4) navamsha dispositor in kendra | Strong |
| **Gajakesari Yoga** | Jupiter in a kendra from the Moon | Moderate |
| **Parivartana Yoga** | Two planets in mutual sign exchange — treated as if conjunct in both houses | Strong |

## Pancha Mahapurusha Yogas (BPHS Ch. 39)

Mars/Mercury/Jupiter/Venus/Saturn in own sign or exaltation AND in a kendra from lagna or Moon:
- **Ruchaka** (Mars) — Aries/Scorpio/Capricorn in kendra
- **Bhadra** (Mercury) — Gemini/Virgo in kendra
- **Hamsa** (Jupiter) — Sagittarius/Pisces/Cancer in kendra
- **Malavya** (Venus) — Taurus/Libra/Pisces in kendra
- **Sasa** (Saturn) — Capricorn/Aquarius/Libra in kendra

## Dhana Yogas (BPHS Ch. 37) — Wealth

| Yoga | Rule | Strength |
|---|---|---|
| **Dhana Yoga (2nd-11th lords)** | Lords of 2nd and 11th conjoin, exchange, or aspect | Strong |
| **Dhana Yoga (Lagna-2nd-11th)** | Lagna lord associated with lords of 2nd and/or 11th | Moderate |
| **Lakshmi Yoga** | Lord of 9th in own/exaltation sign in kendra or trikona, with Venus strong | Strong |

## Arista Yogas (BPHS Ch. 38) — Afflictions

| Yoga | Rule | Strength |
|---|---|---|
| **Kemadruma Yoga** | No planet (except Sun/nodes) in 2nd or 12th from Moon AND no aspect on Moon. Cancelled if Moon or a planet is in a kendra from lagna | Strong |
| **Balarishta** | Moon afflicted by malefics in 6th/8th/12th with weak lagna lord — danger in childhood | Strong |
| **Daridra Yoga** | Lord of 11th in dusthana with malefics, or weak lagna lord in dusthana | Moderate |
| **Kuja Dosha** | Mars in 1st, 2nd, 4th, 7th, 8th, or 12th from lagna/Moon/Venus. Cancelled if Mars is exalted, own sign, with benefic, or spouse also has dosha | Moderate |

## Nabhasa Yogas (BPHS Ch. 35) — Planetary distribution patterns

| Yoga | Rule |
|---|---|
| **Rajju** | All planets in movable signs (Aries, Cancer, Libra, Capricorn) — wandering, travel |
| **Musala** | All planets in fixed signs (Taurus, Leo, Scorpio, Aquarius) — determined, fixed |
| **Nala** | All planets in dual signs (Gemini, Virgo, Sagittarius, Pisces) — skilled in many arts |
| **Gada** | All planets in two adjacent kendras — industrious, wealth |

## Chandra (Moon) Yogas (BPHS Ch. 35)

| Yoga | Rule | Strength |
|---|---|---|
| **Sunapha** | Planet (not Sun) in 2nd from Moon — self-earned wealth, intelligence | Moderate |
| **Anapha** | Planet (not Sun) in 12th from Moon — health, fame, spiritual inclination | Moderate |
| **Durudhara** | Planets (not Sun) both in 2nd and 12th from Moon — great wealth and status | Strong |
